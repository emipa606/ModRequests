Vanilla Expanded Framework:
- The Fae tribe can only appear in nature-dense biomes.
- The Fae tribe will have their own custom settlements, filled with unique treasures.
- Items from various VE mods will appear as loot in fae settlements or in the inventories of fae pawns.
- The Last Fae Standing scenario will have a unique starting structure.
- Weapons have been specified as to if they can be used with shields or not.

Alpha Animals: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1541721856
- The Anima Rod is now craftable, by using 200 wood log, 2 huge anima pearls and 10 gold.
- With Giddy Up 2 installed, members of the fae tribe may ride meadow aves, grey coated mouflons and royal aves. Fae faction leaders may ride overgrown or anima colossuses.
- The fae tribe will use chameleon yaks and meadow aves to carry their tradeable goods.
- Certain items will appear as loot in fae settlements, as well as in the inventories of fae pawns.
- The Last Fae Standing scenario will always start with a tamed Anima Colossus.
