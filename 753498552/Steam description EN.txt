Allows you to host, entertain and recruit visitors. 

[h1]Description[/h1]
Have you been wondering what the point of visiting factions is? Look no further, this mod is the answer.

[b]- Convince visitors to join your faction![/b]
[b]- Increase faction relationships![/b]
[b]- Treat visitors well by providing them with rooms, guest beds and joy activities![/b]
[b]- Sell them junk by placing it in their area![/b]
[b]- Happy guests will help out with your chores![/b] [img]http://ufo2.site44.com/new.png[/img]

Savegame compatible.

Can be found [url=http://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/753498552/350542683199135492/]HERE[/url].

[h1]Known incompatible mods[/h1]
- Any mod that gives guests animals (or slaves) 
- Colony Manager (you will need to start a new game)
- No doomed newbies
- RimSlaves 2.0
- GeneticRim
- ClosedDoors
- Custom storytellers that are not fully / correctly implemented (guest tab won't show) 

[h1]Reporting bugs[/h1]
Should you encounter any issues, press Ctrl-F12 to upload your log and paste the link and a description of what went wrong [url=https://github.com/OrionFive/Hospitality/issues]HERE[/url]. If possible, provide a screenshot of the error, your debug log and an explanation of what happened. This will speed up fixing the problem tremendously.

In 90% of all cases, the problem is a conflicting mod or a corrupted installation on your side.
